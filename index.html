ABOUT US

SK Children Foundation is a non-profit organisation (NGO) working around the year to provide free and quality education to underprivileged children. We at SKCF believe in Right to Education to all the children irrespective of their background. Founded in 2016, we have been providing children with free education. Over the course of five years, we have impacted over 1000+ lives, creating a conducive environment for their growth and progress. Through our flagship event ‘Rangmanch’, we also provided the children with a stage to showcase their talent. We organise myriad of activities for the children such as picnics, fitness class etc. where their comprehensive progress is given the highest priority. Prompted by the teachers and enforced by the volunteers, our NGO’s environment encourages them to discover and comprehend more than what is homogenized by the school walls. Together we strive hard towards making the children academically and culturally skilled to represent themselves in the society hand in hand with their privileged counterparts.
